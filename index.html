Knollwood Village Civic Club

For nearly 70 years, the Knollwood Village Civic Club has been instrumental in keeping our neighborhood healthy, attractive, and safe. Thanks to the annual contributions of its residents, the KVCC enforces the Deed Restrictions, funds the Security Patrol, maintains the street medians, welcomes new residents, liaisons with city anc county officials, and more. Building & Deed Restrictions

Knollwood Village is a deed-restricted community. We take the restrictions seriously. Before you consider building or remodeling, check the Deed Restrictions for your property and submit your plans to the Architectural Control Committee for approval. You will get timely, personal support.
